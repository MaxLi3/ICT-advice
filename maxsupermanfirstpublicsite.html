Tech advice, best workstations to buy

So, the first thing to consider is the price. If you're after a cheap workstation, definitely use a HP Pavillion one. But, if you are a pro user, there are a few things to think about. So, you first want to think if you want a HP one or Lenovo. If you choose HP then there will be two options for a lighter workstation. Same as Lenovo. Of course, if you are a basic user then that's the HP Pavillion. If you want a powerful desktop and do not care about the weight, that is the HP Z1 Tower. Want a light, powerful, small one? Z2 mini. Slightly more powerful but one that's still light? Z2 SFF. Even more powerful? Z2 Tower. Wanna rack workstation? ZCentral 4R. Want a powerful workstation for true pros? Z4. Even more performance? Z6. Max performance? Z8. But, if you choose Lenovo then, if you are a pro but don't do too much pro stuff, and/or don't like heavy things, then get a ThinkStation P350 Tiny. If you want exellent performance in a slim package, then that's the P350 SFF. But if you want outerstanding, mind-blowing performance and you do not care about the weight, then get a ThinkStation P350 Tower. So here is my verdict on which one to buy:

  • #1: Lenovo ThinkStation P350 SFF, for pros that like lighter workstations, tied with Z2 SFF and Z2 mini
  • #2: Lenovo ThinkStation P350 Tiny, for pros that don't do much pro stuff and like really light workstations, tied with Z2 tower, Z4 Tower, Z6 Tower and Z8 tower which are all about performance, but Z2 and Z4 Tower are less expensive ones.
  • #3: Lenovo ThinkStation P350 Tower, for pros that want mind-blowing performance, tied with Z1 Tower, which has good performance for a great price
  • #4: HP Pavillion for people who want a smooth, inexpensive workstation
  • #5: Other brands. They break easier than HP and Lenovo workstations at a simmilar price.
